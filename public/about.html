Simplifying Rentals & Repairs Across Western Australia

Moovin is an all-in-one digital platform based in Perth, WA, designed to transform the rental and home service market. We connect tenants, landlords, and real estate companies with trusted repair professionals — streamlining the rental process and improving property management.
